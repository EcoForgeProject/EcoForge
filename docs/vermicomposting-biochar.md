## Vermicomposting Loop + Biochar Integration

- Mix: 5–10% biochar by volume (start low) with worms + bedding  
- Charge: Soak biochar in worm tea 7–14 days  
- Benefits: Worm reproduction boost, NH₃ adsorption, pH buffer, 10–15% extra nutrient retention  
- Output: Charged castings → soil amendment for greens  
- Mars bonus: Long-term carbon sequestration
